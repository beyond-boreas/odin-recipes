Pelmeni

Russian Pelmeni

Description

Pelmeni are a traditional Russian meat-filled dumplings. Pelmeni are a common comfort food, and large batches can be frozen then quickly boiled or fried on demand.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp all-purpose flour

Filling:

  • 18 oz. ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Various other ingredients
