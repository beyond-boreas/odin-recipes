Pelmeni

Pelmeni served with sour cream and dill

Description

Pelmeni are a traditional Russian meat-filled dumpling dish. Pelmeni are a common comfort food, and large batches can be frozen then quickly boiled or fried on demand.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp all-purpose flour

Filling:

  • 18 oz. ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Various other ingredients

Instructions

  1. Call your Babushka.
  2. Ask her how she's doing - is she staying healthy? Do her knees hurt? Does the neighbor's cat keep pooping in her garden? Make sure to spend at least fifteen minutes on this step before proceeding to the next.
  3. Request a bag of frozen pelmeni.
  4. Wait one to two weeks.
  5. Pick up the pelmeni she dropped off at your mom's house.
