Gator Water

Homemade Gator Water has a lovely green hue. This recipe also fluoresces under blacklight - perfect for parties!

Description

Gator Water is a Ugandan cocktail popularized by the Wakaliwood breakout hit Who Killed Captain Alex (2010).

Ingredients

  • Alligator (fresh)
  • Water
  • Lemon
  • Mint Leaf (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Ask the alligator for permission (written consent is the gold standard).
  2. Pick up the alligator by the tail.
  3. Squeeze the alligator's torso until gator juice is secreted into preferred cocktail glass.
  4. Safely return alligator to its natural habitat.
  5. Squeeze full lemon into glass.
  6. Dilute with water until desired light-green hue is achieved.
  7. Garnish with mint leaf and serve alongside hor d'oeuvres.
