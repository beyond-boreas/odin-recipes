Muddy Hands

Muddy Hands can be enjoyed by all ages.

Description

Muddy Hands is a earthy dessert traditionally eaten using the hands.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dirt
  • Your hands!

Instructions

  1. Add the dirt and water into a mixing bowl.
  2. Using a whisk, beat the water into the dirt until combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into a deep-walled baking sheet. Spread evenly.
  4. Carefully place both hands onto the baking sheet until they are covered with the mixture.
  5. Enjoy!
